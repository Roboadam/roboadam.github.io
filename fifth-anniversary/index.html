Our Story

Adam and Colleen are thrilled to be getting married! Colleen grew up in Alexandria, Virginia and Adam grew up in Winchester, Virginia. Both have lived in the northern Virginia area for many years. Colleen works as a school counselor and Adam works as a computer programmer. The two met online and hit it off right away! They love spending time with friends and family, going out to good restaurants, cooking, and taking walks out in nature.

Adam popped the question over Labor Day weekend during a hike in nearby Great Falls, Virginia. The hike was great and the weather was perfect. Of course Colleen said yes and after proposing, Adam took out some champagne he had been secretly carrying in his backpack. After finishing the rest of the hike, the two had a nice picnic together. Adam and Colleen are so excited to celebrate their wedding with families and friends.
